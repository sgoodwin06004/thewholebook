Context
Matters

Every excerpt stripped of context. Every keyword flagged without meaning. Every child denied a story that might have saved their life.

They Rate. We Respond.

Across America, AI-powered tools are being used to generate book challenge reports — scanning for keywords, producing "ratings," and flooding school districts with paperwork designed to overwhelm librarians and remove books from shelves.

The Whole Book is a counter-database. For every book flagged by groups like Moms for Liberty and tools like BLOCKADE, we document the full picture: the literary merit, the awards, the professional reviews, the readers whose lives were changed.

Because a paragraph torn from its context is not a review. It's a weapon. And context is the antidote.

What We Do
A three-step counter-response to every politically motivated book challenge in America.

We shine a light on the darkness that is book banning and censorship. By providing all the information we can find on a challenged book, we give you the information you need to decide for yourself.

The censors look at keywords and out-of-context quotes. They are afraid of profanity, sex, gender, and ideas that challenge their narrow viewpoints. Here you learn the truth.

01
We Document the Challenge

When a book is challenged or removed from a school or library, we record it — the district, the state, the tool or group behind the challenge, and the excerpts used to justify it. Every ban has a paper trail. We follow it.

Evidence-based
02
We Restore the Context

Tools like BLOCKADE strip passages from their narrative context to manufacture outrage. We document the full picture: professional reviews, literary awards, reader impact statements, and what these books actually say when read in full.

Full context
03
We Build the Counter-Record

Our reviewers submit detailed reports for every flagged title. The database is publicly searchable — so parents, educators, librarians, and journalists can find the real story behind any challenged book within seconds.

Publicly searchable
