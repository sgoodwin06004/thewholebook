Context
Matters

Every excerpt stripped of context. Every keyword flagged without meaning. Every child denied a story that might have saved their life.

They Rate. We Respond.

Across America, AI-powered tools are being used to generate book challenge reports — scanning for keywords, producing "ratings," and flooding school districts with paperwork designed to overwhelm librarians and remove books from shelves.

The Whole Book is a counter-database. For every book flagged by groups like Moms for Liberty and tools like BLOCKADE, we document the full picture: the literary merit, the awards, the professional reviews, the readers whose lives were changed.

Because a paragraph torn from its context is not a review. It's a weapon. And context is the antidote.
